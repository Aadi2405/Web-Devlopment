About GTBIT

Guru Tegh Bahadur Institute of Technology (GTBIT) is one of the leading private engineering institutes in Delhi, affiliated to GGSIPU and approved by AICTE. Established in 1999, GTBIT is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering innovation, discipline, and professionalism.

Academics

GTBIT offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering and Technology. The academic environment at GTBIT is rigorous, focused on research, and supports skill development to meet industry needs.

  • B.Tech in Computer Science, IT, ECE, EEE, AI & ML
  • Industry-aligned Curriculum
  • Experienced Faculty and State-of-the-art Labs

Departments

GTBIT is home to several departments, each equipped with specialized labs and faculty. Inter-departmental collaboration and project-based learning are highly encouraged.

  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Applied Sciences and Humanities

Admissions

Admissions to GTBIT are conducted through GGSIPU CET. The institute also admits students via lateral entry and management quota as per university norms.

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with PCM, CET-qualified
  • Admission through GGSIPU counseling
  • Management Quota (as per norms)
  • Scholarships for meritorious students

Placements

GTBIT has a strong placement record, with top recruiters visiting the campus every year. The Training and Placement Cell assists students in soft skills training, resume building, and interview preparation.
